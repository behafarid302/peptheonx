PeptheonX develops peptide-driven nanobiotechnology and computational frameworks for molecular design in applied research and industrial contexts.

About

PeptheonX operates at the intersection of nanobiotechnology and peptide-based molecular systems. We focus on building computational frameworks that enable de novo design, rational design, evaluation, and translation of peptide technologies across applied research and industrial settings.

Our approach combines physical insight, data-driven modelling, and system-level thinking to support scalable and defensible innovation.
